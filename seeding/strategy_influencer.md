# Influencer & Challenge Strategy (Phase 2-3)

To amplify the organic reach, we will implement a micro-influencer outreach and a community-led challenge.

## 1. #AniMatchChallenge
**Goal**: Encourage users to share their results and tag friends.
**Mechanics**:
- Users post their result cards on Twitter/X or Instagram Stories with `#AniMatchChallenge`.
- **Incentive**: Weekly "Card of the Week" featured on the AniMatch landing page ticker.
- **Viral Trigger**: "I matched with [Character Name]! Tag a friend who looks like [Another Character Name] to see if they match."

## 2. Micro-Influencer Outreach
Target micro-influencers (1k–50k followers) in the subculture niche who have high engagement with anime fans.

**Target Segments**:
- **Vtubers (Indie)**: Great for live reaction content.
- **Anime News/Meme Accounts**: High reach for viral-style content.
- **Cosplayers**: Can pose with their "match" character.

**Outreach Message (Draft)**:
> 안녕하세요 [인플루언서 이름]님! 
> AI가 사진을 분석해 운명의 애니 캐릭터를 찾아주는 'AniMatch' 개발팀입니다.
> [인플루언서 이름]님의 평소 애니메이션에 대한 애정과 팬들과의 소통이 저희 서비스와 잘 어울린다고 생각하여 연락드렸습니다.
> 
> 저희 서비스를 한번 체험해 보시고, 팬분들과 결과를 공유해 주시면 서비스 성장에 큰 도움이 될 것 같습니다!

## 3. Monitoring & Engagement
- Monitor the `#AniMatchChallenge` hashtag daily.
- Retweet and engage with user results to boost visibility.
- Use GA4 `share_click` data to see which influencer drive the most traffic.
